"Steve Hamilton is a great bass player." - John Pattituci

Steve Hamilton is jazz bassist and composer hailing from Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. He has been active on the Canadian music scene for over 35 years as a Jazz bassist, and has also played with Symphony Orchestras, Chamber groups and New Music ensembles as well as Musical Theatre.
